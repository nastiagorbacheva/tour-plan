Issue was only that Lift was not in working and we were allotted to 3rd floor and amenities articles were in corner of gallery which were giving bad feeling. Stayed 23 Jun, 2017

We selected this hotel based on the reviews and it did not disappoint. It exceeded our expectations! The location is excellent, the building is charming, and the staff went out of their way to make us feel at home. One morning we had an early meeting time for a tour and the staff packed us a to-go breakfast to eat on the tour bus. We also enjoyed a bottle of champagne on their terrace to celebrate our wedding anniversary. Highly recommeded!

Peter Thompson

Stayed 11 Jan, 2018

Our travel agent recommended this hotel as a reasonably priced family owned hotel. It was charming from the front door all the way up the stairs to our hotel room. The large windows opened to the air and a beautiful street sight of casading flowers and cobblestones below. The furniture in the room had old world charm, the hallways were filled with decor from the past and beautiful photographs. The dining area was comfortable, with a wide variety of buffet breakfast items, Americano and Italiano together, and also featured made to order cafes and lattes. Every employee more charming than the next. You won't regret a stay at this lovely hotel.
